THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO HOWIE

My Top 3 Movies

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King (2003)

The Return of the King is the best of the trilogy. Great audio and visual effects, cinematography, the incredible creatures, and magnificent battles.

Gladiator (2000)

A wonderful story that shows the character of a former Roman General who was sent into slavery and how he free himself against the corrupt emperor.

Troy (2004)

Best epic war and adventure film on the assault on Troy.
